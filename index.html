Project Portfolio

Virtual Feed

A Virtual Reality Simulation to Suppliment Antenatal Education

Unity | Blender | Leap Motion | Oculus
Virtual Feed is the first prototype of an interactive breastfeeding simulation designed as a research instrument/experience prototype to explore the potential of interactive and immersive technology in supplementing antenatal education. This work is a part of my PhD at KU Leuven. The work has been submitted to ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2021 - Interactivity Track.
Project Portfolio

Interactive Surgical Dental Implant Planner

An interactive tool to help dental surgeons/clinical technicians plan dental implant and design surgical guide.

Mevislab | C++ | Python | PythonQT
Surgical Implant Planner is an interactive tool designed in cooperation with and deployed at UZ Leuven to help surgeons and clinical technicians plan surgical implant, design and 3D print surgical guide. This project was a part of my Master's thesis at KU Leuven.
